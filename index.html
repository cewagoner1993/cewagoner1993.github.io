We're so glad you're here!

There are few spots in Evansville that so well encapsulate the city's virtues: its vibrancy and its eclecticism. But Leather Strop Barber Shop, on Evansville's West-Side, is all of those things. All of our barbers are local to the west side of Evansville or close surrounding areas. Our shop is only months old and things are already in full swing!

Years of experience have been brought to this barbershop to make it more than a simple place to get a haircut, beard trim or old fashioned hot towel straight razor shaves. It is where young men come learn the ways of manhood, men come to talk with ease and both are made to look like true gentleman when they leave. Come treat yourself to a place of serenity that can only be found at Leather Strop Barber Shop and treat yourself to the true gentleman experience

Leather Strop Barber Shop keeps their fingers on the pulse of the industry, which allows them to not only stay current, but also be innovative and dictate trends. The experience begins when the potential and returning clients walk through the door, choose their barber and delve into local or world events, personal matters or take time to reflect quietly while their barber creates magic.
